NATURAL LIP CARE PRODUCTS

Our lips are exposed to copious stresses in everyday life. The sun exposure, cold temperatures, dry heating air, dehydration, and smoking steal the moisture of our lips' sensitive skin. Unlike the rest of our face and body, our lips neither have sebaceous glands nor a protective layer of oil....As a result, it turns dry, chapped, cracked, dark, and loses its naturally soft and supple texture. Lip care routine becomes a troublesome process. To curb these lip concern, Bella Vita Organic have curated lip care products range to get kiss-worthy lips anytime, anywhere. Our products are made with conditioning butter and natural ingredients that glide over lips to lock in moisture while the unique scrub lip treatment smooths the way for an instantly soft, kissable pout.
